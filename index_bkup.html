About

--ME--
I am a life-long learner and a creative engineer leveraging various programming languages and technologies.

I’ve made this space to document my goal of documenting something new for each week in 2016.

--1 PROJECT A WEEK --

I’ve laid out this space as a place to document my past, current, and future technology projects. Starting in 2016 I plan to document and upload an average of 1 project per week. By 2017, I plan to have 50 projects documented. I am giving myself 2 “Bye” weeks in the 52 weeks of 2016.

---RESHAPE & GIVE BACK --

By using this space to document my projects in 2016 (and beyond), I hope to reshape my understanding of the implicit trade-offs, strengths, and synergies between different technologies. I also believe as the space evolves there will be more opportunities to give back and contribute to the open-source and online communities that have both enabled and breathed life into my past technology successes.

---PORTFOLIO / CONSULTANT ROLES--

I plan to use this space as a portfolio of technical proficiency for independent technology consulting roles. I don’t like being asked what my ideal role would look like…. Only because the real answer is “collaborating on a team to deploy big data analysis algorithms – to a business technology stack – which all needs to be completed while hacking an Arduino.” I hope that the breadth of concrete technology implementations, documented here, is a better answer….

---FEEDBACK—

As a life longer learner I am eager for critical feedback, or contributions, on any of my implementations. None of these projects will be perfect and I am always interested in learning better methods and best practices for implementation.
